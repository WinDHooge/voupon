About Voupon

Voupon (noun.) - Coupon + voucher. A virtual gift card, that is incredibly thoughtful.

Starting out as a small study project founded by three Java Developers, Voupon grew to the successful company it is today. We wanted to create something that is hassle-free, intuitive to use, and can still bring so much to the table.

Voupon is a fast and easy way for you to offer completely customisable gift vouchers to your online customers. Easily access your own, or your company’s information, create Voupons with a value of your liking, add themes, set expiry dates and so many other things.

All you have to do is sign-up, set up your monthly payment of only €15/month, and we’ll handle the rest!
